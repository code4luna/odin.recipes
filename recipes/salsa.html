Lamp Salsa

Ingredients

  • 2 large cans of whole, steamed tomatoes.
  • 1 large sweet onion
  • 1 bunch of cilantro
  • 1 bunch of green onions
  • 1 small can of jalapenos
  • 1 small bottle of lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons of Kroger brand garlic salt

Directions

  1. Open the tomato cans and place the contents into a large bowl.
  2. Rough chop all of the vegetables.
  3. Open the jalapenos and dump all of the liquid into the bowl. Add jalepenos to taste.
  4. Add vegetables to bowl and with a hand blender, blend until salsa.
  5. Add garlic salt to taste.
